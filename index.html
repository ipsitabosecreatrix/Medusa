Medusa

The Medusa is a precision-engineered rotary tattoo machine inspired by the flowing, muscular movement of a serpent. Its scaled body echoes the fluid motion of a snake, giving the machine both visual identity & ergonomic balance.

At the heart of the Medusa lies a high-performance DC motor, delivering consistent torque and smooth power transfer. The adjustable cam wheel allows stroke length customization ranging from 1.5 mm to 5.5 mm, enabling artists to fine-tune the machine.

The Medusa is engineered for versatility, balance, and control - a fusion of mechanical precision and organic inspiration.
